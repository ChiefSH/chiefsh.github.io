Who I Am

My name is Fred Miles and I am a .NET Developer. I began studying to become a developer after I graduated from Miami University in 2015 as I had found that I did not enjoy working in my field of study. I was directed towards 1150 Academy in Carmel Indiana and it was there that I found my new passion. Working with computers had always been something I really enjoyed but I had never really taken the steps to get involved in it until recently. Through 1150 I have learned a great deal about developing apps and websites in the .NET framework and gained a proficiency in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and of course C#.
